About Yoga

There are many different path of Yoga, but first of all what is Yoga? Yoga is a collection of spiritual techniques and practicies integrating mind, body and spirit to achive a state of oneness with the Universe! What is normally thought of Yoga in the West is Hatha Yoga. One of the many style of Yoga. Every yoga type has their own techniques and approaches, but they all lead to the same goal.

Hatha Yoga

The union of mind, body and spirit through a practice of yoga postures, yoga breathing, body gestures and internal cleansing. These physical practice used to purify the body and energy. Read more about Hatha Yoga on Wikipedia

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga practice is equal spiritual and physical. It is all about realising the kundalini energy in your body. These classes are very intense, work your core and breathing with fast moving, invigorating posture and breath exercises. Read more about Kundalini Yoga on Wikipedia

Which Yoga is good for you

Regardless what is your goal or what would you like to achive, yoga is good physical and mental exercise. Yoga is for everyone when you want to practice, age or wheight or what you work doesn't matter, you can do it anywhere anytime. If you injury or medical condition, first better to consultate with a physician or a qualified health care professional.

How to start

If you are new to yoga or out of shape, better to begin with gentle practiceuntil you build up your flexibility and strength. When your fitness level is better or you already practiced, you chould start with hatha yoga. Once you know the basic postures you can explorer vinyasa.
