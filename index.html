About Yoga

There are many different styles of Yoga, but first of all what is Yoga? Yoga is a collection of spiritual techniques and practicies integrating mind, body and spirit to achive a state of oneness with the Universe! Yoga practice is thousands of years old, a place to learn the best technics to breathe, stretching your body, and to find a connection between your mind and body, reaching the mindfulness. Yoga has many benefits, balance, flexibility, improves strengts. Helps with back pain relief. But still Yoga is not about pushing yourself, reaching goals quickli, but about loving and feeling your body, relaxing your mind. So this is why the most important step is, to find the best Yoga type for you! One of the most popular style in the West is Hatha Yoga. But this is just one of the 11 types of Yoga, that you might have even heard about or tried. Every yoga type has their own techniques and approaches. But all of them bring together physical and mental disciplines to achive a peacful body and mind. We are here to help you to find the best type of this sport that millions of people love! We hope this will be something that you are looking for to do day by day, a sport, that you can enjoy without goals, rolling down your Yoga mat with excitment, and rolling it up with peace in your mind!

Hatha Yoga

The union of mind, body and spirit through a practice of yoga postures, yoga breathing, body gestures and internal cleansing. These physical practice used to purify the body and energy. The purpose is to relief from three types of pain: physical, enviromental and spiritual. For beginners is the best yoga because of its slow pace, and is great when you just start Yoga practice. Best time to practice it is in the morning before breakfast. Read more about Hatha Yoga on Wikipedia

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga practice is equal spiritual and physical. It is all about realising the kundalini energy in your body. These classes are very intense, work your core and breathing with fast moving, invigorating posture and breath exercises. The majority of the physical postures focus on activity of the spine and selective pressurization of body points and meridians. Read more about Kundalini Yoga on Wikipedia

Which Yoga is good for you

Regardless what is your goal or what would you like to achive, yoga is good physical and mental exercise. Yoga is for everyone if you want to practice, age or wheight or what you work doesn't matter, you can do it anywhere anytime. It should be something that you want to, and enjoy to do! It is mostly depends on your personality. If you looking for something more spiritual, you do like singing, unlock your energie, then Kundalini Yoga is for you! Kundalini means "life force energie", clears your aura, and gives good vibes, positive mindset. If you are looking for less spiritual type of Yoga with a good stretch, but not too intensive, then Hatha Yoga could be your cup of tea! It´s a nice mix of more styles of the physical side of Yoga, but still includes meditation. Often recommended for beginners. If you injury or medical condition, first better to consultate with a physician or a qualified health care professional.
